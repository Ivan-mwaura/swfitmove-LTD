Before You Book

How do I make a booking on your website?

To book a transfer, simply visit our website and select your desired transfer type. Then, fill in your travel details, including departure and arrival locations, date, and time. You'll receive a confirmation upon completing the booking.

What information do I need to book?

To complete your booking, you'll need to provide your full name, email address, phone number, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Payment details are also required to confirm your booking.

Can I book a transfer for multiple stops?

Yes, we offer multi-stop transfers. During the booking process, you can specify your additional stops, and we will customize the route for you.

How do I pay for my transfer?

You can pay for your transfer online using our secure payment gateway. We accept all major credit and debit cards. We also offer mobile payment options for added convenience.

Before You Travel

What luggage entitlement do I have?

Each passenger is entitled to one medium-sized suitcase and one carry-on item. Additional luggage may incur extra charges. Please notify us in advance if you have more luggage or oversized items.

Where will I meet the driver when I arrive?

Your driver will meet you at the designated pickup location specified in your booking confirmation. For airport transfers, they will be at the arrivals hall with a sign bearing your name.

If you have trouble finding your driver, please contact our customer support team immediately. They will assist you in locating your driver or arranging an alternative solution.

Can I change the vehicle if my group size changes?

Yes, if your group size changes, you can adjust your vehicle choice before your scheduled transfer. Please inform us at least 24 hours in advance to make the necessary arrangements.

How long will the transfer take?

The duration of your transfer depends on the route and traffic conditions. We provide estimated travel times during the booking process, but please allow for flexibility in case of delays.
